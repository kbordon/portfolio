Background

I finished a B.A. in Japanese Studies many many years ago, mostly at that point to just get a degree. In retrospect, I would've liked to take a break before high school rather than rushing into university, but hindsight is just that.

Since graduating, I've tried to return to school for second bachelor's or a master's in mind while making a living as a barista. It hasn't exactly been the easiest, but at the very least, I feel my ability to create microfoam is quite adequate.

As much as I love not having to pay more than $5 for 12 oz latte whenever I want, being a barista is, at least for me, not the fulfilling job.

Epicodus appealed to me because of that focus on people like me, looking for change and eager to learn about something new.
